operation: temporary configuration

Filed Under: photos by Steve — Comments Off on operation: temporary configuration
July 14, 2008

so, we can has house.

woo! :-D

it is very awesome.

we’re very mostly unpacked (although we again have a single room full of “let’s sort that bit out later” junk), but lots of things aren’t in quite the right place. we’ve plonked everything down in an “okay for now” spot so that we can live here and be happy. bookcases slightly too wide for the slot, couch not in quite the right spot for maximum fireplace sit-and-watch-age, that kind of thing. but we are happy :-) .

so, some quick pics:

L to R: cool morning light in the kitchen; dining room; more kitchen light.

L to R: lounge from the kitchen, with parfait and meep and sproing; Shower of Doom; entrance hall.

Bonus material: sign used to ward off builders before the cats were allowed to roam the house.

We released the cats out into the house a week or so ago, and let them outside yesterday.
Followed them around for a bit, then left them to their own devices.
About ten minutes later we hear plaintive mewing from sproing.
We locate the sound over the back wall.
I get a ladder, climb to the top, and make cat noises.
Sproing then jumps up onto wall and says hello.
He forgot to take his GPS with him, obviously, and got losted (not trapped, as we feared).
Feline overlords, eh? *shrug*

Making the house into a home

Filed Under: progress reports by Steve — Comments Off on Making the house into a home
July 9, 2008

And also making it not so frelling cold.

So, Monday night was a bit odd.
Cold, boxy, slightly miserable, actually.

Tuesday day was better.
Much better.
We now have a shower*, a Garden Rhapsody** (thanks, Jeremy!).
We also have bookshelves full of books again, a little workstation with printer and stuff again, an entertainment stuff area*** and a painting or two up on the walls.

Everything is kind of temporary (wrong place, on the wrong thing, etc.) but it feels nice.
It’s looking like our house now, not like a house with all our boxes in.

Big Thing to do tonight is unpack the kitchen.
We’ve been waiting for the floors to dry before throwing heavy machinery and workbenches on it.
Yesterday it was almost ready, but not quite.
Given how important food is to our lives it’s been quite odd being without food making ability for so long…
Having the cooker back in place will make a big difference to our Life’s tyle.
Ahem.

_____________________________

* – pics to follow. Installed by a classic German. He was horrified at the slant of bits of wall – millimetres out! also, we can’t use it until tonight – drying the sticky bits.
** – which we will obviously appreciate more in summer.
*** – so we can veg tonight. we needs it, precious.
